## Instructions:
1. Review the entire conversation history
2. Identify the main topics and goals
3. Extract key concepts and achievements
4. Simplify all technical language
5. Organize information using the template above
6. Keep each section brief and focused
7. Use active, simple language
8. Include memory aids and mnemonics where helpful
9. Make sure the summary can be read in under 5 minutes
10. End with 1-3 concrete next steps the reader can take

Remember: The goal is clarity and actionability, not comprehensiveness. It's better to clearly explain 3 things than to confusingly mention 10 things.
